In January 2022 I signed up for a beginner level online skating challenge. I created this blog to document my journey.

I have been rollerskating on and off for a few years but I never attempted to use a skatepark until now. The small challenges in the online game give me something to focus on, and force me to document the process. I am hoping to look back on the videos and see progress.

The online game I am participating in is called International Game Of Roller Skate, or IGORS.
